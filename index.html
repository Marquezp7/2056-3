Legenday Pokemon Dialga

Dialga the temporal Pokemon. Also Dialga is a Dragon/Steel type pokemon. Dialga can warp time by either speeding it up, slowing it down, or stopping it altogether. It can also travel through time to visit the past, present, and future. It is said that time started moving when Dialga was born and will continue flowing as long as Dialga's heart still beats.
